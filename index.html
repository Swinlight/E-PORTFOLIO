ABOUT MY PASSION

Watching Movies

I am also highly passionate about watching movies, and it goes deep within me like colorful threads in a beautiful tapestry. Not only is movie-watching my hobby but, rather, a consuming relationship with the art of narrating stories, the essence of visual images, and the importance of such an experience to the human soul. Above all, I am a movie-passionate person because the art requires great effort and skills in making it gorgeous. For example, I am constantly looking for new movies to watch and enjoying exploring them.

Coding

To me, my love for coding is like a fire burning deep within me. It enhances my sense of curiosity and innovation while also enabling me to view the digital space in multiple dimensions. Since I wrote my first “Hello, World!” programmer, I’ve been intrigued by how simple lines of code can turn thoughts into opportunities while also moulding lives. More than just a technical ability, coding is a type of language for me. It is a piece of paper on which I write my ideas, viewpoint, and aspirations. As I sketch a visually stunning user interface, optimize an algorithm for speed, or solve a turbulent insect, each line of code symbolizes my artwork expression- proof of what I wish to accomplish.

Playing Basketball

To me, basketball is not only a game but also life ; it brings joy, unity and improves personal character. In that atmosphere, I am totally drawn into what is happening around me as I concentrate my energy in giving it my all for the love of playing.
